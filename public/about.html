About Our Company

Founded in the heart of Dublin, Dublin Dog Walkers was created with a simple mission: to provide the highest standard of care for the city's canine companions. We understand that a dog is more than just a pet, they are a member of the family. That is why we offer comprehensive care that goes beyond just a walk. From essential daily exercise and specialized fitness routines to personalized attention, we ensure every dog receives the care they need to thrive.

Our Team

What sets us apart is our people. Our team consists of highly qualified professionals who are not only trained in canine behavior and first aid but are also deeply passionate about animals. We treat every dog as if they were our own, bringing energy, patience, and love to every session.
